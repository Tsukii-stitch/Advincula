About Us

We are a tiny group devoted to finding loving homes for abandoned animals and caring adopters. Our purpose is to link homeless dogs with people willing to open their hearts and homes. By arranging adoptions, we hope to give these animals a second opportunity at a happy, healthy life while also fostering important ties between dogs and their new families.

Visit Us at:

Noah's Ark Animal Dog and Cat Sanctuary / Shelter, 74AH+65W, Unnamed Road, Mabalacat, Pampanga
