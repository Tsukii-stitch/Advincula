Common FAQs

  • 1. Can I return a pet if it doesn’t work out? Yes, we have a return policy if the adoption isn’t the right fit. We ask that you notify us as soon as possible so we can find a better match for the pet.
  • 2. Are the pets vaccinated? Yes, all pets are fully vaccinated and spayed/neutered before they are adopted.
  • 3. Can I adopt more than one pet at a time? Yes, but each pet requires a separate application, and we will review if your home can accommodate multiple pets.
  • 4. Do you offer trial periods? Yes, we offer a two-week trial adoption for most pets. During this time, you can bring the pet home to see if it’s the right fit. If not, the pet can be returned with a full refund of the adoption fee.

Eligibility and Requirements

Age Requirement

You must be at least 18 years old to adopt a pet.

Home Environment

We may require a home visit for certain pets, particularly larger dog breeds or animals with special needs, to ensure your space is suitable.

Pet Compatibility

If you have other pets at home, we’ll need to confirm they are up-to-date on vaccinations and spayed/neutered.
